x About x

Welcome to my website! My name is Sam Fewkes and I am currently a junior at Illinois State University. I started my educational journey at ISU as a Nursing Major, and after months of working as a CNA and getting a look into the industry I knew it wasnt for me...

After finding out nursing wasnt my thing I started thinking back to high school and what I enjoyed during my time there. I remembered all the engineering courses I took and how I loved being creative, but I knew I didnt want to be an engineer. I was having trouble pin pointing what exactly I wanted to pursue until my aunt talked to me. My Aunt works at ISU as a the Assistant to the Director of the College of Fine Arts, and she talked to me about joining said major.

I thought about it for a while and it all made sense. I have always loved video games (heck I even stream them now!) and to combine my love for being creative with video games was a match made in heaven! Every since joining the CTK program I have never been happier with school and my interest has only grown. Last year I created my first ever video game (its a little rough around the edges, but its a start) and the classes i'm taking now are teaching me about sound design, video editing, web page development, and so much more!

Ambitions

Goals are an essential part of becoming successful and I have a lot of them!

  • 1. Join a video game production studio thats working on a game im interested in.
  • 2. Gain years of work experience and establish good connections/friendships.
  • 3. Start my own studio to work on games I come up with (I have so many franchise game ideas).
  • 4. While working towards the previous goals I am going to stream myself gaming.
